THE WAY OUT OF CRISIS
JOIN THIS COMPLIMENTARY WEBINAR NOW!
Gain Insights and Solutions to Transform Your Organisation

Dr Kevyn Yong, Chief Learning Officer, SIM and his guest will discuss on How to Thrive in Times of Crisis - how companies can begin making that shift - from mobilising during the crisis, to stabilising in the new environment, to strategising for what is next.

In this session, you'll discover:
• How large corporations can adapt to the new normal by leveraging bold solutions and frameworks
• How to identify the big adjustments that need to be taken on the Corporate and Community level to thrive in the new normal
• How to close the capability gap to capture key regional opportunities during these uncertain times
This is the first instalment of a 6-episode series that examines the 4 critical influences of D.A.S.H.+ framework - Design Thinking, Agile and Transformational Thinking, Systems Thinking, Hyper Performance Strategies and Leadership (+) - to help you and your organisation emerge stronger.
WHO SHOULD ATTEND
Developed for leaders, senior executives and anyone feeling tremendous pressure to maintain high levels of performance despite the current challenging business climate and for anyone that would beneﬁt from practical skills to help them navigate through these uncertain times.
